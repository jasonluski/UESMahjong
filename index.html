About

Learn American Mahjong in the heart of the Upper East Side. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to sharpen your game, lessons are tailored to your pace in a warm, welcoming setting.

What to Expect

Sessions cover the fundamentals — reading the card, hand types, the Charleston, and proper etiquette. Small group and private one-on-one lessons available. All materials provided.
